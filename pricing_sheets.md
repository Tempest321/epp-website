Great value entry point. Sits within senior contractor range while delivering AI/ML specialization. Easy ROI story for clients—delivers $550K+ savings for ~18% of Year 1 value.

### $150K / 3 months — $156/hr
Mid-range AI/ML specialist positioning. Credible rate that signals expertise without sticker shock. Strong margin while remaining accessible to mid-market contractors ($30-50M revenue).

### $200K / 3 months — $208/hr
Premium positioning within standard AI/ML consultant range. Appropriate for enterprise clients or when delivering proprietary technology. Justified by compressed timeline and specialized domain expertise.
